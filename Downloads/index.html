Face/Off ▼
Face/Off is a high-speed, high-stakes game of trivia for two to six players. Each player starts with a fixed amount of time and must answer questions to stop their clock and start their opponent's. The first player to run out of time is eliminated from the game.

A license is required to run this game. Please contact royal_flush in the Discord to acquire one. A valid Twitch account will be required to validate your license.
